The Best Movies According to Asadbek

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A powerful story about hope and friendship set in a prison,
based on a Stephen King novella.

Inception (2010)

A mind-bending sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan,
exploring dreams within dreams and the nature of reality.

Parasite (2019)

A South Korean dark comedy and thriller that delves into class disparity and societal issues,
winning the Academy Award for Best Picture.
